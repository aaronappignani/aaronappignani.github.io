My Cameras

Olympus Infinity Zoom 70

This is my Olympus Infinity Zoom 70, which really kick-started my desire to pursue photography as my primary hobby/interest. Easily the best eBay purchase of my life. This camera has a decently sharp lens compared to most film cameras of the time, and really excels when shooting black-and-white, or really low ISO film. I typically use this for vacations or trips out of the town I live in, where the extra cost of film is justified.

Canon PowerShot SX120IS

This is my Canon PowerShot SX120IS. I bought it for $40 from a rich lady in Greenpoint in February of 2024. I use this camera for video recording mostly -- it gives off that Emma Chamberlain "recorded in 4x3 and kinda grainy" vibe. I sometimes use the microphone on this, since it actually works pretty well at getting that vintage camcorder style mic.

FujiFilm FinePix A340

This is my Fujifilm FinePix A340. I bought this from a guy in Astoria who was in the process of retiring and becoming a snowbird. I hope he's doing well! This is my "daily driver" camera of sorts -- If I'm randomly snapping a picture, it's probably with this camera. I personally LOVE the saturated colors and the slightly cloudy/blurry vibe that it gives off. Unfortunately, this doesn't take video at all.

Polaroid Now+

My aunt gave me this Polaroid for Christmas of 2022! It works well, but isn't something that I use very often. Polaroid film is just so expensive for the volume of shots you get to work with, especially compared to digital or even regular 35mm roll film.

FujiFilm Instax

This camera is my fiance's, but I use it pretty often too! This is typically just used for capturing nice dinners in the house, and things like that.
