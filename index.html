My Cameras

Olympus Infinity Zoom 70

This is my Olympus Infinity Zoom 70, which really kick-started my desire to pursue photography as my primary hobby/interest. Easily the best eBay purchase of my life.
This camera has a decently sharp lens compared to most film cameras of the time, and really excels when shooting black-and-white, or 200 ISO film.
I typically use this for vacations or trips out of the town I live in, where the extra cost of film is justified.

Canon PowerShot SX120IS

This is my Canon PowerShot SX120IS. I bought it for $40 from a rich lady in Greenpoint in February of 2024.
I use this camera for video recording mostly -- it gives off that y2k, recorded in 4:3, 2006 YouTube video vibe.
I sometimes use the microphone on this, since it actually works pretty well at getting a specific, low-quality, home-video sound.

FujiFilm FinePix A340

This is my Fujifilm FinePix A340. I bought this from a guy in Astoria who was in the process of retiring and moving down South. I hope he's doing well!
This is my "daily driver" camera of sorts -- If I'm randomly snapping a picture, it's probably with this camera.
I personally LOVE the saturated colors and the slightly cloudy/blurry vibe that it gives off. It has a film-like quality, but in its own way entirely.
Unfortunately, this doesn't take video at all.

Polaroid Now+

My aunt gave me this Polaroid for Christmas of 2022! It works well, but isn't something that I use very often.
Polaroid film is just so expensive for the volume of shots you get to work with, especially compared to digital or even regular 35mm roll film.

Olympus FE-170

This is the first digital camera I ever bought -- and the first I ever broke. I purchased it on eBay in 2022, using it to take pictures of my friends when I was bartending their frat parties.
This camera is what got me into both digicams and photography as a whole. I would highly reccommend buying one of these, if they weren't like fifty dollars on eBay without the expensive and proprietary memory cards.
I also have some extremely grainy video from this camera.
